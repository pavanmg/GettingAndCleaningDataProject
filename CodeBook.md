## Activity Labels

* `WALKING` (value `1`): subject was walking during the test
* `WALKING_UPSTAIRS` (value `2`): subject was walking up a staircase during the test
* `WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS` (value `3`): subject was walking down a staircase during the test
* `SITTING` (value `4`): subject was sitting during the test
* `STANDING` (value `5`): subject was standing during the test
* `LAYING` (value `6`): subject was laying down during the test

## Variables and dataframes used 


* `activityLabels` : contains names of all the activities corresponding to the activity number laster used to rename the number with actual name of the activity
* `all_data_mean_std_selected` : Selected data set after apply filter for mean and std 
* `all_data_mean_std_selected.melted` : melted data with subject and activity selected for use in columnar DBs
* `AVG_data_perUser_perActivity_col_selected` : Final data report generated after calculating mean for all the measurements of interest, that was selected from the filter.
* `dataColNames_feature_selected` : List of colnames that contain mean and std
* `dataColNames_features` : List of all col names
* `dataColNames_mean_std_filter` : List of all col numbers with mean and std
* `test_subject` : subjects in the data that were selected for testing 
* `training_subject` : subjects in the data that were selected for training
* `X_data` : Measurement data have both test and training 
* `X_data_subset_mean_std_selected` : Measurement data have both test and training but only columns of interested after applying filter and subsetting
* `X_test` : Measurement data for test  subjects
* `X_train` : Measurement data for training  subjects
* `y_data` : Activity data for X_data
* `y_test` : Activity data for X_test
* `y_train`: Activity data for X_train
